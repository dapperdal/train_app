# Train App

## About the App

This is a personal Android application designed for Darren to track information about his favorite train stations. The app is tailor-made to provide quick access to the schedules and statuses of specific stations he is interested in.

## Purpose

The main goal of this app is to simplify the process of checking train times and station information for personal, everyday use. Instead of navigating complex public transport apps, this app will provide a direct and simple interface to the information Darren needs most.

## Features (Placeholder)

*   **Favorite Stations List:** Quickly view a list of your most-used train stations.
*   **Real-Time Schedules:** Check upcoming train departure and arrival times.
*   **Simple Interface:** A clean and straightforward design focused on readability.

***

*This application is intended for personal use and is not designed for public distribution.*
