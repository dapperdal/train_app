# Train App

## About the App

This is a personal Android application designed for Darren to track information about his favorite train stations. The app is tailor-made to provide quick access to the schedules and statuses of specific stations he is interested in. Home station to work station. No need to click through to choose the location - it's all based on where you are at, it will auto update. Optionally you can choose to switch it if you like.

## Purpose

The main goal of this app is to simplify the process of checking train times and station information for personal, everyday use. Instead of navigating complex public transport apps, this app provides a direct and simple interface to the information Darren needs most.

## Features

### Train Schedules
* **Auto-Location Detection:** Automatically detects if you're near Leigh-on-Sea or Fenchurch Street and shows relevant trains
* **Real-Time Schedules:** Check upcoming train departure and arrival times with live updates
* **Delay Information:** See which trains are delayed and by how many minutes
* **Service Disruptions:** View any current disruptions affecting the C2C line
* **Pull to Refresh:** Swipe down to get the latest train times

### Journey Tracking
* **"I'm Onboard" Button:** Tap to start tracking your journey when you board a train
* **Progress Tracking:** Visual progress bar showing how far along your journey you are
* **Stops Remaining:** See how many stops until your destination and the next station name
* **Arrival Countdown:** Large display showing minutes until you arrive
* **Delay Updates:** Real-time delay information during your journey

### Arrival Alerts
* **2-Minute Warning:** Get alerted 2 minutes before arriving at your destination
* **Audio Alert (Bluetooth):** Voice announcement "Arriving in 2 minutes" when Bluetooth headphones are connected
* **Vibrate Option:** Silent vibration alert if you prefer or don't have headphones connected
* **Customisable:** Toggle alerts on/off and choose between audio or vibrate

### Weather at Arrival
* **Weather Forecast:** See the weather at Fenchurch Street when you'll arrive
* **Rain Warning:** "Bring an umbrella!" notification when there's rain expected
* **Precipitation Probability:** Percentage chance of rain at your arrival time

## Technical Details

* **Platform:** Android (min SDK 26 / Android 8.0)
* **Train Data:** National Rail Darwin API via Huxley2
* **Weather Data:** Open-Meteo API (free, no API key required)
* **Stations:** Leigh-on-Sea (LES) and Fenchurch Street (FST)
